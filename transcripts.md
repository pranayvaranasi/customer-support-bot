
### 1. in sop

**Scenario:** Customer asks a standard question covered by the SOP, and the AI smoothly transitions into lead qualification without hallucinating.

```markdown
**Customer:** What are your Botox prices?

**AI:** Botox treatments at Bloom Aesthetics start from £200. Have you visited Bloom Aesthetics for treatments before?

**Customer:** No, I haven't.

**AI:** Welcome! We'd love to have you. Do you have a specific date or time in mind for your visit, or would you prefer to schedule a free consultation first?

**Customer:** A consultation next Tuesday at 2 PM.

**AI:** Wonderful. You can finalize your booking for next Tuesday at 2 PM via our WhatsApp or website. Please keep in mind we require a 24-hour notice for cancellations. Is there anything else I can assist you with today?

```

### 2. out of sop

### 3. escalation sentiment

**Scenario:** Customer expresses frustration and mentions a medical complication. The AI detects the strict escalation triggers and stops the automated flow.

```markdown
**Customer:** I came in for fillers last week and my lip is super swollen and it hurts. I'm really upset with the results.

**AI:** [ESCALATE: Medical complaint and frustration] I am so sorry to hear that you are experiencing pain and swelling, and I completely understand your concern. Because this is a medical question, I am transferring you to a human agent and our clinical team immediately so they can assist you properly.

```

### 4. lead qual

**Scenario:** The AI successfully asks the structured qualification questions step-by-step before finalizing the conversation.

```markdown
**Customer:** I'd like to book some fillers.

**AI:** We would be happy to help you with that! Fillers at Bloom Aesthetics start from £250. Before we proceed, have you visited Bloom Aesthetics for treatments before?

**Customer:** Yes, I came in last year.

**AI:** It's great to welcome you back! Are you looking to schedule a consultation first, or do you have a specific date in mind for your filler treatment?

**Customer:** I know what I want, I just need an appointment for this Friday morning.

**AI:** Perfect. You can finalize your booking for this Friday morning via our website or WhatsApp. Just a quick reminder of our policy: we require 24 hours' notice for any cancellations. Can I help with anything else?

**Customer:** Nope, that's it. Thanks.

**AI:** You're very welcome! Have a wonderful day, and we look forward to seeing you on Friday.

```
